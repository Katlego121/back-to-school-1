Background

Back to Basics is a Saturday school that aims to drill the fundamentals of Mathematics and English targeting Grade 7-9 learners. This is to allow learners to acquire and be proficient on the basic elements of Maths and English. Reason being that this is difficult for most teachers to achieve during normal contact time due to a host of reasons.

Many learners sadly are not able to cope or keep up with the pace of syllabus coverage therefore become demoralised and despondent. It is our aim to not only help such learners but also to assist in refining those who are not necessarily at risk but still require a little T.L.C in order for them to really flourish.

Exclusive Summary

Unesco lists South Africa as having a literacy rate of 93% but‚ beyond the mechanical ability to identify words‚ the picture is much bleaker. According to research released by the University of Pretoria‚ eight out of 10 Grade 4 pupils “still cannot read at an appropriate level”.

South Africa was placed last out of 50 countries in the Progress in International Reading Literacy Study (PIRLS) which included nearly 320‚000 children globally. The survey also found that reading scores have not improved since 2011.

The above paints a gloomy picture of South Africa that is why we felt a need to do something to reverse this scourge. Back to Basics is a non-profit organisation that was formulated after observing a pressing need in the community for learners to get access to an additional means of educational support to underprivileged learners. Our organisation was also formed to assist in improving learners’ ability.

Vision

To be the best Saturday School in Gauteng East that prides itself in providing educational excellence.

To empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them as life-long learners.

Mission

To deliver content in a fresh and compelling way by integrating technology in our lessons such as projectors, viewing YouTube videos or sending e-books to learners to keep our lessons interesting.

Objectives

To provide a learner friendly environment

To provide participant learners from our targeted communities inspiration for personal growth.

To expose participants to support systems that enables them to cope with academic pressures

To empower the youth and the community with character and behavioural skills.

Target Group

Our core target market is Tsakane Extension 8 High School learners as well as surrounding primary and high schools.
