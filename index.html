Why choose us

Overall goal is the provision of tuition and academic support to grade 7, 8 and 9 learners

Vision

To be the best Saturday School in Gauteng East that prides itself in providing educational excellence.

To empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them as life-long learners.

Mission

To deliver content in a fresh and compelling way by integrating technology in our lessons such as projectors, viewing YouTube videos or sending e-books to learners to keep our lessons interesting.

Objectives

To provide a learner friendly environment

To provide participant learners from our targeted communities inspiration for personal growth.

To expose participants to support systems that enables them to cope with academic pressures

To empower the youth and the community with character and behavioural skills.

Target Group

Our core target market is Tsakane Extension 8 High School learners as well as surrounding primary and high schools.
